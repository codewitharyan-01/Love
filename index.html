Design Philosophy

Every decision
was deliberate.

  • Why no screen?
    Screens demand attention. Wispa gives it back. When you don't need to look, you're present — in conversations, in moments, in life.
  • Why so small?
    4.5cm diameter. 1.5cm thin. It disappears — on your keychain, your bag, your collar — until you need it.
  • Why voice-first?
    Voice is the most natural human interface. It's faster than typing, richer than tapping, and asks nothing of your hands or your eyes.

Private by design.

We built Wispa to serve you, not to study you. Your voice is never stored. Your data is never sold.

🔒
No persistent recording
Wispa listens only when activated. Audio is processed in real-time and never saved to any server.
🛡️
Encrypted in transit
All communication between Wispa and the cloud uses end-to-end encryption. Always.
🚫
Zero data selling
We have no advertising model. Your data has no commercial value to us — only to you.
